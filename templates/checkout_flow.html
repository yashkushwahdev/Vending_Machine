Payment Checkout Flow

Our purchasing process is designed to be simple and secure. Here is how it works:

  • Product Selection: User selects a product from the website or vending interface.
  • Payment Display: The system displays the payment option (QR / UPI / gateway).
  • Payment Completion: User completes the payment using their preferred method.
  • Payment Verification: Payment is verified through the payment gateway.
  • Dispensing: Upon successful payment, the vending machine dispenses the selected product.

In case of payment failure or product not being dispensed, users can contact support for assistance or refund at sudhanshupatel517@gmail.com.
